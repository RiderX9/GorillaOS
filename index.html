// Why GorillaOS

Built Different

GorillaOS strips away everything you don't need and keeps everything you do. Powered by the CachyOS-optimized kernel for real-world performance gains out of the box.

⚡
CachyOS Kernel

The optimized CachyOS kernel delivers measurably better performance, lower latency and improved responsiveness compared to stock Linux kernels.

🦍
Zero Bloat

No preinstalled junk, no telemetry, no nonsense. Every package included has a reason to be there. Your system, your rules.

🔄
Rolling Release

Built on Arch Linux. Install once and stay up to date forever. Always on the latest software with no major version upgrades needed.

🖥️
Your Desktop, Your Choice

GorillaOS ships with GNOME, KDE Plasma, and Cinnamon. Choose your preferred environment during installation — all three are fully supported.

🛠️
Arch Foundation

The full Arch Linux ecosystem at your fingertips. Access the AUR, pacman, and decades of community knowledge and documentation.

🎯
Guided Installer

A simple, custom installer walks you through setup in minutes. No Arch expertise required — just answer a few questions and go.

// Choose Your Environment

Three Desktops.
One Installer.

During installation GorillaOS gives you a choice. Pick the desktop that suits your workflow — you can't go wrong with any of them.

Option 1 🔵
GNOME
Clean · Modern · Focused

GNOME offers a distraction-free, touch-friendly desktop with a clean aesthetic and powerful extensions ecosystem. Perfect if you want a modern, streamlined experience.

  • Activities overview & app grid
  • Wayland-native by default
  • Huge extensions library
  • GNOME 50 included
Option 2 🟦
KDE Plasma
Powerful · Customizable · Fast

KDE Plasma is endlessly customizable, feature-rich and surprisingly lightweight. If you like having everything exactly the way you want it, Plasma is for you.

  • Unmatched customization
  • KDE Plasma 6.6 included
  • Wayland & X11 support
  • Powerful built-in apps
Option 3 🟩
Cinnamon
Familiar · Elegant · Reliable

Cinnamon delivers a traditional, intuitive desktop experience that feels instantly familiar. Great for users who want productivity without a learning curve.

  • Classic taskbar & menu layout
  • Cinnamon 6.6 included
  • X11 with great hardware support
  • Lightweight & responsive
